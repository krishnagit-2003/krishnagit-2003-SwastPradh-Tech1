At SwastPradhi MedTech, we are committed to empowering surgeons and healthcare teams across multiple specialties with reliable, cutting-edge technology. Our precision-engineered Light Source and fluid management systems are designed to meet the diverse clinical demands of minimally invasive and endoscopic procedures, enhancing surgical outcomes while improving workflow efficiency.

Laparoscopy

Enhanced Visualization & Controlled Operative Fields

Laparoscopic surgery requires high-quality illumination and effective management of the surgical field to ensure safety and accuracy. Our Light Source provides high-intensity, adjustable brightness with superior color rendering to distinguish anatomical structures with clarity. It supports both 2D and 3D endoscopic systems with uniform illumination and reduced heat emission.



Our simultaneous suction & irrigation Pump ensures a controlled and sterile isotonic solution flow, helping to:

- Maintain a clear surgical field
- Optimum solution of fluid
- Variable flow rates with efficient turnover time of the operating room

Applications:

- General surgery (cholecystectomy, appendectomy)
- Colorectal proceduref
- Bariatric interventions

Benefits:

- Less fatigue due to consistent lighting
- Fewer interruptions due to visual obstruction
- Precise pressure and flow settings tailored to each procedure

Urology

Safe & Efficient Urological Endoscopy

Urological interventions like TURP, URS, and PCNL require precise control over irrigation pressure and volume. Our Irrigation Pump includes customizable flow and pressure settings that reduce the risk of fluid overload and intrarenal pressure spikes, which can compromise patient safety. The Light Source ensures deep illumination into the urethra, bladder, and kidney pelvis for improved endoscopic navigation.



Clinical Uses:

- Transurethral resection of the prostate (TURP)
- Percutaneous nephrolithotomy (PCNL)
- Ureteroscopy (rigid/flexible)
- Cystoscopy

Key Features:

- Programmable flow modes (continuous, pulsatile)
- Integrated pressure feedback
- Compatibility with bipolar and monopolar instruments

ENT (Otorhinolaryngology)

Clarity in Compact Surgical Spaces

ENT surgeries involve compact anatomical areas and demand tools that deliver excellent visibility without thermal risk.



Our Light Source is optimized for narrow-lumen endoscopes, offering:

- Homogeneous light distribution
- Crisp visualization for sinus, nasal, laryngeal, and otologic surgeries

The Irrigation Pump facilitates constant fluid flow during endoscopic sinus surgeries (ESS), enhancing:

- Mucosal visibility
- Hemostasis
- Reduced post-op complications due to cleaner operating fields

Relevant Procedures:

- Functional Endoscopic Sinus Surgery (FESS)
- Tympanoplasty
- Nasal polypectomy
- Microlaryngoscopy
